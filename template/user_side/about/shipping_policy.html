How Long Does It Usually Take To Deliver An Order?

From The Date Of Purchase You Can Expect Delivery Within 4-5 Days. Free Shipping On Orders Above Rs. 3,999

What To Do If I Didn't Receive A Tracking Number And Still Hasn't Passed 5-5 Business Days?

Upon Dispatch Of The Order, You Will Receive A Tracking Number Along With An URL, Within 2-5 Days. You Can Use That URL To Track Your Order.

Why Isn't My Order Showing Up On The Tracking Page?

Sometimes It Can Take Up To 48 Hours For Your Order To Show Up On The Tracking Page. Please Allow 3-5 Business Days Before You Contact Support. Sometimes Tracking Information Is Not Shown Immediately.

I Only Received One Of The Items In My Order. Where Is The Rest?

Sometimes Packages Split Into Multiple Shipments. All Of Your Items Are On The Way, Don't Worry. You'll Receive Each Item In Your Shipment, Don't Worry. You'll Receive Tracking On Your Other Goods As They Ship.

Will You Keep My Credit Card Information On File?

No, We Ensure Your Payment Information Is Handled By Our Payments Provider Razorpay. All Your Payment Information Is Handled By Our Payments Provider Razorpay.

Can I Use An Online Credit In-Stores?

Unfortunately, Seplash Online Credits Can Only Be Used On Www.Seplash.In, And Cannot Be Used In-Store.
